About Us

At Nourish Nudge, we're on a mission to combat hunger and food waste by harnessing the power of surplus. With a deep-rooted commitment to social responsibility, we rescue excess food from various sources and redistribute it to those facing food insecurity. Our innovative approach not only addresses immediate hunger needs but also contributes to environmental sustainability by reducing food waste. Through collaborative efforts with donors, volunteers, and partners, we're creating a ripple effect of positive change, nourishing communities and fostering hope for a brighter, more equitable future.
